Meet Aether

Aether is our flagship product — a portable, real-time air quality monitor built to empower individuals and organizations with clean air insights. It tracks pollutants like PM2.5, CO₂, VOCs and more, providing instant data through an intuitive mobile app.

Designed to be affordable, portable, and user-friendly, Aether makes air quality monitoring accessible for everyone — at home, at work, or on the move.
